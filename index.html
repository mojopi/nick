Timothy Helps Out

Timothy lived on an island. He had everything he could ever want or need. Life was good for Timothy, until one day when he saw something in the distance. It was someone holding a big net. This person wanted to catch Timothy!

Timothy tried to run away and hide in a bush, but the person caught him and took him back to the boat.

The person was getting ready to sail away but then saw that Timothy was crying and nervous. He felt guilty about trying to steal Timothy and let him free.

Timothy was so happy to be free he cried tears of joy. Then, he laid down and took a nap.

The man sailed back home with a new love for nature.

He realized that he wanted others to love nature as much as he did, so he decided to teach kids about respecting nature and being kind to animals.

A few years later he went back to the island to visit his old friend Timothy who had changed his life.


The End.
